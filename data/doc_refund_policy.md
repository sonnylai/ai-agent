title: Refund policy for orders
text: |
  Refunds: Orders can be refunded only if status == 'paid' and refund requested within 30 days from created_at.
  Refund calculation: full refund minus processing fees. For multi-item orders, refund is per item if item-level return requested.
  Business rule: 'cancelled' orders have no refunds.
